| <a href="https://youtu.be/u6bP2Ikr9Xs?t=7592" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">2:06:32</a> | Has `Allah` always meant `God`, or is it a pagan divine name? | He said it is the generic Arabic Semitic word for God, used even by Arabic Christians, and not the name of a pagan deity. |

| <a href="https://youtu.be/u6bP2Ikr9Xs?t=9856" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">2:44:16</a> | Is there a book he recommends on biblical numerology? | No. He affirmed that some biblical numbers are symbolically important, especially `7`, `12`, and `40`, but said most numerology books overextend the evidence. |
