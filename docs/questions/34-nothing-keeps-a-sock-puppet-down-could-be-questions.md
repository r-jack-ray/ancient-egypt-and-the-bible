| <a href="https://youtu.be/g8-BQFODrd8?t=203" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">3:23</a> | If all humans descend from Noah after a global flood, should languages have a common ancestor? | He said language change over long stretches of time makes that unsurprising in principle, and he did not see the issue as fatal either to flood models or to evolutionary models. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/g8-BQFODrd8?t=429" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">7:09</a> | Does Potiphar sending Joseph to prison instead of killing him show he doubted his wife's accusation? | No. He said Joseph was being remanded for royal judgment, because ordinary officials were not simply free to execute a slave on their own authority. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/g8-BQFODrd8?t=627" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">10:27</a> | Is the ark of the covenant in Ethiopia? | He said no. He treated the Ethiopian claim as late legend and said even the object reported at St. Mary of Zion was described as a medieval replica. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/g8-BQFODrd8?t=995" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">16:35</a> | Are there archaeological clues for Sodom and Gomorrah? | He rejected both Ron Wyatt's claims and the Tell el-Hammam identification, saying Sodom may still be undiscovered. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/g8-BQFODrd8?t=1213" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">20:13</a> | What does he think of the Gabriel Revelation stone and its messianic language? | He treated it as interesting Second Temple period material if genuine, but not as canonical or especially threatening to New Testament claims. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/g8-BQFODrd8?t=1523" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">25:23</a> | Did Amalek have a bad reputation outside the Bible? | He said we lack extra-biblical evidence for Amalek, so there is not enough information to answer that confidently. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/g8-BQFODrd8?t=1644" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">27:24</a> | Why do Psalms say "Arise, O Lord"? Does that echo Amun-Re? | He said the Hebrew here is more a call to action than a solar or ascension image, so he did not think Psalm 3:7 was really about Amun-Re. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/g8-BQFODrd8?t=1861" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">31:01</a> | Why do Deuteronomy 10:6 and Numbers give different-looking versions of Aaron's death? | He suggested Deuteronomy 10 may preserve a gloss or brief catch-up notice rather than a competing full narrative. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/g8-BQFODrd8?t=2265" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">37:45</a> | How does Psalm 82 fit with John 10:34? | He treated Psalm 82 as divine-council language and said John's use depends on that broader biblical pattern rather than a simplistic one-to-one category. |
