Live Stream #253: The Grassy Nome Theory

| <a href="https://youtu.be/_6WmbdDHC8s?t=556" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">9:16</a> | Is it sinful to abuse the `weaker brother` idea by trying to hold other people's private behavior hostage? | Yes. He said flaunting freedom can be sinful in public, but using that text to control someone else's private conduct is also a misuse of the passage. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/_6WmbdDHC8s?t=697" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">11:37</a> | Is there evidence for a 40-year desolation of Egypt under Nebuchadnezzar in Ezekiel 29? | He said there is some evidence of Babylonian looting and disruption in Egypt's heartland, but not strong evidence for a literal total desolation. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/_6WmbdDHC8s?t=930" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">15:30</a> | Who was the woman scholar mentioned for prehistoric Anatolia? | He clarified that he meant Erica Hughes as a specialist in prehistoric Anatolia, not an antediluvian or pre-flood scholar. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/_6WmbdDHC8s?t=1648" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">27:28</a> | Who were the `Philistines` in Abraham and Isaac's time? | He treated that label as a retrojection for later readers and thought the people in view were Canaanites on the coastal plain, not actual later Philistines or `Paleo-Hyksos`. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/_6WmbdDHC8s?t=1745" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">29:05</a> | Why do the borders in Genesis 15 seem much larger than later Israelite territory? | He said the promise is to Abraham's descendants broadly, not only to Israel, so it includes other related peoples spread across a much wider region. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/_6WmbdDHC8s?t=1997" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">33:17</a> | What are your thoughts on the Kalašma language tablet? | He said only that it appears to reflect a Luwian dialect related to Hittite and did not claim detailed familiarity with it. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/_6WmbdDHC8s?t=2135" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">35:35</a> | May Christians eat foods such as pork and shrimp? | Yes. He treated the dietary restrictions as part of the fulfilled ritual law rather than binding on Christians. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/_6WmbdDHC8s?t=2304" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">38:24</a> | What do you think of Graeme Hancock, Mark Armitage, and `big archaeology` conspiracy claims? | He rejected the conspiracy framing, arguing archaeology is underfunded and that such claims confuse fields and ignore the actual evidence problems. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/_6WmbdDHC8s?t=2843" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">47:23</a> | What happens to Christians who die before Christ's return? | He said believers go directly to be with the Lord in heaven before the final resurrection. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/_6WmbdDHC8s?t=2940" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">49:00</a> | Do Christians pray in heaven and in the new heavens and new earth? | He said prayer as a means of conforming our will becomes unnecessary in heaven because there is direct access to God. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/_6WmbdDHC8s?t=3230" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">53:50</a> | Will people need to cover their eyes before God like angels around the throne? | He said probably not, treating the angelic wing-covering more as modesty language than as a response to personal sinfulness. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/_6WmbdDHC8s?t=3439" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">57:19</a> | Do Jewish Christians still need to keep the ritual law? | No. He said the ritual law is fulfilled in Christ, even if some Jewish believers still choose to observe parts of it. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/_6WmbdDHC8s?t=3535" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">58:55</a> | Have any Revelation events happened in this century? | He said not so far. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/_6WmbdDHC8s?t=3581" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">59:41</a> | Will pets and other animals go to heaven? | He tentatively thought some animals do, while qualifying that not all animals necessarily share the same destiny or relationship to humans there. |
