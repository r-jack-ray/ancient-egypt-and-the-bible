| <a href="https://youtu.be/fVPZXw6Vbt0?t=447" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">7:27</a> | Has later research materially changed the Egyptian loanword argument for the historicity of the Exodus? | He says no. New work has added detail, but it has not materially altered the overall argument. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/fVPZXw6Vbt0?t=584" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">9:44</a> | What is the gift of tongues in 1 Corinthians 14, and are they angelic tongues? | He treats tongues as real language, not gibberish; in Second Temple thought that could include angelic language, while "mysteries" are speech not understood by ordinary hearers. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/fVPZXw6Vbt0?t=2022" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">33:42</a> | How do you correct someone who rejects all scholarship as man-made doctrine? | He says you probably will not break through if the person rejects every tool of learning; even an English Bible already depends on scholarship. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/fVPZXw6Vbt0?t=4566" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">1:16:06</a> | Why does the Bible not mention the Anatolian Hittite Empire? | He says the empire had already collapsed by the time Israel entered the land, so the Bible encounters later Neo-Hittite states instead. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/fVPZXw6Vbt0?t=5220" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">1:27:00</a> | Is there value in Manetho? | Yes, but cautiously. He says Manetho is useful while also being late and preserved only through later writers whose copies often disagree. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/fVPZXw6Vbt0?t=8205" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">2:16:45</a> | What would you recommend for someone seeking formal biblical education while working and raising children? | He says formal schooling is still better than self-teaching for a formal credential, but family obligations remain the priority and narrow what is realistic. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/fVPZXw6Vbt0?t=12191" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">3:23:11</a> | What were ancient Israelite family units like, and how would they view modern nursing homes and pushing kids out at 18? | He says Israelite clan life was multigenerational and wealth-preserving, so they would have seen that modern pattern as both cruel and wasteful. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/fVPZXw6Vbt0?t=15554" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">4:19:14</a> | When should someone leave a church? | His answer is that you leave when God calls you elsewhere, and that can include leaving either a bad church or a good one for ministry reasons. |
