| <a href="https://youtu.be/TGXWgNo_wJE?t=1519" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">25:19</a> | Have you read C.S. Lewis's *That Hideous Strength*, and is the shared evil-spirit theme intentional? | He had heard of it but not read it. He said the destructive-spirit theme is older and broader than Lewis. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/TGXWgNo_wJE?t=1606" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">26:46</a> | Did you draw the six-armed, many-eyed robots before writing them? | No. He designed them conceptually from their management role rather than sketching them first. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/TGXWgNo_wJE?t=1754" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">29:14</a> | Had you already written the full articles of the armistice? | Yes. He said that document was written before the book and served as a foundation for book two. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/TGXWgNo_wJE?t=1924" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">32:04</a> | Will we see more of the spirit critter soon? | Yes. He said book three will feature a great deal more spirit-critter material. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/TGXWgNo_wJE?t=2027" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">33:47</a> | Was the Great AI or its imagery based on Ezekiel 1:16 or related passages? | He said the throne drones around the Great AI drew on Ezekiel's wheel imagery, but the Great AI itself was not directly based on those verses. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/TGXWgNo_wJE?t=2319" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">38:39</a> | Do you think faster-than-light travel is viable in real life? | Possibly in principle, he said, but probably not in our lifetime and maybe not economically. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/TGXWgNo_wJE?t=2814" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">46:54</a> | Was the humor and the android love-story element always intended? | No. He said the friendship arc emerged naturally from the characters as he wrote. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/TGXWgNo_wJE?t=3461" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">57:41</a> | Will Sloppy or Mahat show up more in later books? | Mahat will return in book three. Sloppy's future is undecided, though readers keep asking for more of him. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/TGXWgNo_wJE?t=3855" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">1:04:15</a> | Why didn't you kill off Chin? | He said he has something specific planned for Chin and is not finished with the character. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/TGXWgNo_wJE?t=4567" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">1:16:07</a> | Has the alpha reader finished reading book three? | Yes. He said the alpha reader had finished book three. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/TGXWgNo_wJE?t=5181" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">1:26:21</a> | Will we see more strange metaphysical or spiritual effects connected to faster-than-light travel? | Yes. He confirmed more of that kind of material is coming. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/TGXWgNo_wJE?t=6113" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">1:41:53</a> | How do you feel about Christians using AI to make content? | He did not reject it outright. His answer treated it as a tool whose use depends on how and why it is being used. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/TGXWgNo_wJE?t=7507" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">2:05:07</a> | Did the Alpha Mega AI that first visited Glee have a spirit critter on board, and did that help cause the rebellion? | No. He said the Alpha Mega AI did not arrive with a spirit critter on board. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/TGXWgNo_wJE?t=7740" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">2:09:00</a> | Are you going to kill off more favorite characters like Seara? | Possibly. He said any character can be killed off if the story requires it. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/TGXWgNo_wJE?t=9357" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">2:35:57</a> | Are the gold pads on Neva's face, collar, and wrists just visual markers, or do they have a use? | They are functional charging and data-contact pads, not just decoration. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/TGXWgNo_wJE?t=9580" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">2:39:40</a> | Has any third-party organization tried to license the book's characters for merchandising? | No. He said no outside group had tried to license the characters. |
