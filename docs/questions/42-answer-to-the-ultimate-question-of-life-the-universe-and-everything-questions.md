| <a href="https://youtu.be/DdLF22PXUTY?t=318" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">5:18</a> | In Genesis 18, who are the three visitors to Abraham? | Falk said one is clearly a theophany, while the identities of the other two are not given securely enough to press beyond that. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/DdLF22PXUTY?t=428" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">7:08</a> | How does Mormon polytheism compare with ancient Near Eastern polytheism? | He said Mormon henotheism is structurally very different, especially in its layered cosmology of gods and exalted humans. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/DdLF22PXUTY?t=697" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">11:37</a> | Does "this generation" in Matthew 24:34 require Jesus' return to have been near in time? | No. Falk argued that *genea* has a wider range and in context points to the persistence of the same kind of people and conditions until the end. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/DdLF22PXUTY?t=1152" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">19:12</a> | What does he see as the main problem with the documentary hypothesis? | He thought it does violence to the literary structure of Genesis and unnecessarily dissects features that fit ancient composition. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/DdLF22PXUTY?t=1318" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">21:58</a> | Are ghosts real? | He allowed that unusual spiritual encounters can occur, but said most ghost reports are better explained as deception, misidentification, or special divine exceptions rather than ordinary dead humans roaming freely. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/DdLF22PXUTY?t=1695" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">28:15</a> | How were amulets used in Egyptian mummification? | They were often wrapped into specific places on the body or added among the burial materials to protect and empower the deceased. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/DdLF22PXUTY?t=1951" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">32:31</a> | Is the satan in Job the devil or some other accuser figure? | He leaned toward a prosecutorial accuser role, while allowing that later readers may see continuity with the devil. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/DdLF22PXUTY?t=2454" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">40:54</a> | What is the difference between a clan and a tribe in ancient Israel? | A clan is a smaller kinship unit within a tribe, and Falk stressed that biblical social structure is far more clan-based than many modern readers realize. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/DdLF22PXUTY?t=7721" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">2:08:41</a> | What does he think of Petrovich's work on inscriptions like the Lahun bilingual? | He rejected Petrovich's broader method as circular and unreliable, and did not treat the claims as credible. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/DdLF22PXUTY?t=8105" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">2:15:05</a> | What is the best way to start learning Jewish Palestinian Aramaic? | Learn Hebrew first, then use proper grammars and bound lexicons rather than depending on thin online word lists. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/DdLF22PXUTY?t=8760" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">2:26:00</a> | Can biblical genealogies be used to build reliable timelines? | No. He said clan genealogy, honorific transposition, and the way toledot work make that method unsuitable. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/DdLF22PXUTY?t=8981" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">2:29:41</a> | Did Abraham and Moses actually speak Hebrew? | Probably not in the later classical sense; Falk said Abraham likely spoke Akkadian and related dialects, while Israelite speech became Hebrew through a long creolizing process. |
