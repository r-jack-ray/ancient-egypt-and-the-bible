Live Stream #182: Rolling Dice For The Answers

| <a href="https://youtu.be/6frru028lOc?t=783" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">13:03</a> | In Matthew 1:5, is Rahab the same Rahab from Jericho? | He answered yes, treating the genealogy as referring to Rahab of Jericho and Ruth of the biblical book of Ruth. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/6frru028lOc?t=1214" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">20:14</a> | What is your best advice for beginning writers? | His short answer was to read a lot and write a lot, especially in the genre you want to write. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/6frru028lOc?t=1501" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">25:01</a> | How is a sexually immoral person sinning against his own body in 1 Corinthians 6:18? | He connected it to the body belonging to Christ and being a temple, so sexual immorality misuses what has been bought by Christ and given over to God's service. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/6frru028lOc?t=2412" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">40:12</a> | Do Exodus 14 and 15 directly borrow from the Kadesh inscriptions? | He allowed awareness of the poetic structure and content, but was hesitant to call it straightforward direct borrowing. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/6frru028lOc?t=8131" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">2:15:31</a> | Why do you not hold to Deutero- or Trito-Isaiah? | He said he sees Isaiah more as a deliberately reflected literary whole and is not convinced the standard multiple-Isaiah division is necessary. |
| <a href="https://youtu.be/6frru028lOc?t=11207" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">3:06:47</a> | Does scripture teach that the church has precedence and determines everything because God formed a believing community before written scripture? | He said the church does come first historically, but he framed the relationship as the church, scripture, and the Holy Spirit working together rather than the church unilaterally determining everything. |
